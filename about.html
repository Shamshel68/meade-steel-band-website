What does the steel band do?

In the steel band, we're all about making music with steel pans. We sometimes have special events inside and outside of the school where we share our music. For those who don't know what a steel pan is, it's a musical instrument that's quite popular in Trinidad and Tobago, where it originated. They look like oversized cooking pots with craters in them. A pannist, or someone who plays a steel pan, would hit the craters to make different pitches. Just don't hit them any harder than necessary...
